Framework Cheat Cards

NIST CSF (high-level)

Often used as a flexible, risk-based structure to organize a cybersecurity program. Helpful for maturity and prioritization. Not “one-size-fits-all.”

ISO/IEC 27001 (high-level)

Management system approach (ISMS). Strong for governance consistency and formal structure across the organization. Often chosen when a structured security management system matters.

SOC 2 (high-level)

Common for service organizations needing customer assurance. Focuses on demonstrating controls and evidence over a period of time. Often requested by customers/partners.
